# Home

Hey, thanks for stopping by! 

My name is **Justin Meader** and I am an **IT Systems Engineer** who specializes in **Identity Access Management**. This site serves as a place for me to share my thoughts, interesting tools and solutions I've come across online, and as a repository for public notes around my life and interests.

This isn’t a a traditional personal website. It’s not a blog or a digital résumé. It’s more of a [digital garden](https://ryan.warner.codes/what-is-a-digital-garden/) than anything else —  a sort of personal wiki, and I’m publishing the parts that don’t contain anything I’m not able/willing to share.

I’m still working out an organizational system, but I’ve begun to loosely implement [Tiago Forte’s PARA Method](https://fortelabs.co/blog/para/). Additionally, in the interest of not burying some of the more interesting things I’m working on (interesting *to me*, anyway), I’ve brought a few folders into the main navigation structure.

Not sure where to start? These are some of my current projects and interests:
- If you came here for my IT experience, you’ll likely want to head over to the [[IT|IT]] or [[2. Areas/Identity Access Management/Identity Access Management|Identity Access Management]] pages.
- [[Three Times Faster/Three Times Faster|Three Times Faster]] is a podcast about [[Gundam|Gundam]] that I created with my best friend, [Sean](https://www.spfitzgerald.com/). We haven’t produced any episodes in awhile, but we’re coming back this year (I promise!).
- I’m an avid video game player with an extensive [[Games/Backlog|Backlog]]. I’m also working on a couple of projects involving my favorite games:
	- [[1. Projects/Yharnam Worldbuilding Exercise|Yharnam Worldbuilding Exercise]] — Bloodborne is one of my favorite games of all-time, and I’m incredibly fascinated by the lore of it. I’m also interested in worldbuilding in writing, so I’ve been templating a structure I could use to build my own worlds, and then filling out the structure using Bloodborne’s world of [[Games/Bloodborne/Yharnam|Yharnam]] to see how it holds up.
	- [[1. Projects/13 Sentinels Project|13 Sentinels Project]] — The art and storytelling in Vanillaware’s *13 Sentinels: Aegis Rim* is some of the most incredible in all of video games. I loved it so much that I’m documenting the game much in the same fashion as Bloodborne, but with the additional intention of making a lengthy YouTube video about it.
